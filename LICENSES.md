## Upstream Data

- TESS products originate from NASA/MAST and the SPOC pipeline.
- Gaia products originate from ESA Gaia and DPAC.
- TOI metadata originates from the NASA Exoplanet Archive and ExoFOP-TESS.
- 2MASS, AllWISE, APASS, and other catalog data retain their upstream terms.

Large raw TESS files are not redistributed in the compact archive. Their MAST
identifiers, sizes, and SHA-256 hashes are provided for verification.
